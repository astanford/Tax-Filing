## Rules

### Overall SALT Cap
- The deduction for state and local taxes is limited to **$40,000** ($20,000 if MFS). [Schedule A Instructions (2025), Line 5e]
- This is a combined cap covering all taxes on Lines 5a + 5b + 5c.
- Effective for tax years **2025–2029**. The cap and income threshold increase 1% annually. [OBBBA, H.R. 1]

### SALT Components (Line 5)
You can deduct the **total** of these, subject to the cap:
- **Line 5a:** State and local **income taxes** (or general sales taxes — you choose one, not both) [Schedule A Instructions (2025), Line 5a]
- **Line 5b:** State and local **real estate taxes** [Schedule A Instructions (2025), Line 5b]
- **Line 5c:** State and local **personal property taxes** [Schedule A Instructions (2025), Line 5c]

### Income Tax vs. Sales Tax Election
- You can elect to deduct state/local **general sales taxes** instead of income taxes on Line 5a. You cannot deduct both. [Schedule A Instructions (2025), Line 5a]
- Use actual receipts or the IRS Optional Sales Tax Tables.
- For most filers with state income tax, the income tax deduction is larger.

### What Counts as State/Local Income Tax (Line 5a)
- State and local income taxes withheld from salary (W-2 Box 17/19) [Schedule A Instructions (2025), Line 5a]
- State/local income taxes paid in 2025 for a prior year (e.g., 2024 balance due paid with state return)
- State/local estimated tax payments made during 2025
- Mandatory contributions to state unemployment/family leave funds
- **Do NOT reduce** by any expected state/local refund for 2025
- **Do NOT include** taxes deducted on Schedule C, E, or F

### Real Estate Taxes (Line 5b)
- Deduct state, local, and foreign real property taxes. [Schedule A Instructions (2025), Line 5b]
- Prepayment of next year's property taxes: deductible if assessed and paid in 2025.
- Reduce by any refunds or rebates received.
- Do not include charges for services (trash, water, sewer) unless they are part of the general tax.

### MAGI Phase-Out (High Earners)
- If MAGI exceeds **$500,000** ($250,000 MFS), the $40,000 cap is **reduced by 30%** of the excess. [Schedule A Instructions (2025), Line 5e; OBBBA]
- The cap cannot be reduced below **$10,000** ($5,000 MFS).
- Formula: Effective cap = max($10,000, $40,000 − 0.30 × (MAGI − $500,000))

### Phase-Out Calculation Table (MFJ)

| MAGI | Excess over $500K | 30% Reduction | Effective Cap |
|------|-------------------|---------------|---------------|
| $500,000 or less | $0 | $0 | $40,000 |
| $550,000 | $50,000 | $15,000 | $25,000 |
| $600,000 | $100,000 | $30,000 | $10,000 |
| $600,001+ | >$100,000 | >$30,000 | $10,000 (floor) |

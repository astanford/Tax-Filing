- Maryland 2025 Resident Booklet (Form 502 Instructions), pp. 1–45

## Applicable To
- **Forms:** MD Form 502 (all lines), Form 502SU (subtractions), Form 502B (dependents)
- **Workflow Steps:** Step 2 (Investment Income — U.S. obligation interest), Step 10 (Maryland 502)

## Rules

### Line-by-Line Reference (Key Lines)

#### Income Section
- **Line 1:** Federal AGI (from Form 1040, Line 11). [MD Resident Booklet, Instruction 11]
- **Line 5:** Other additions to income (codes a–t on 502SU). Rarely applicable for W-2/investment filers.
- **Line 6:** Total additions.
- **Line 7:** Federal AGI + additions (Line 1 + Line 6).

#### Subtractions (Form 502SU)
- **Line 8–13:** Subtractions from income. Enter total on Form 502, Line 13. Key codes:
  - **Code ab:** U.S. Government obligation interest — exempt from MD tax. Enter interest on U.S. Treasury securities (T-bills, T-notes, T-bonds, I-bonds, EE/HH bonds). Mutual fund dividends from U.S. obligations: only the portion attributable to U.S. obligation interest is subtractable. **GNMA is NOT exempt.** [MD Resident Booklet, Instruction 13, Code ab]
  - **Code yc:** Union dues (new for 2025). [MD Resident Booklet, Instruction 13, Code yc]
- **Line 14:** Two-income married couple subtraction (Worksheet 13D).

### Two-Income Subtraction (Line 14)
- Available to MFJ filers where both spouses have income. [MD Resident Booklet, Instruction 13D]
- **Maximum: $1,200** or the smaller spouse's Maryland adjusted income, whichever is less.
- Use Worksheet 13D: Each spouse's share of FAGI + additions − subtractions; compare; take the smaller; cap at $1,200.

#### Deduction Section
- **Line 15:** Total subtractions (Line 13 + Line 14).
- **Line 16:** Maryland AGI = Line 7 − Line 15.
- **Line 17:** Deduction — standard OR itemized (see below).
- **Line 18:** Net income = Line 16 − Line 17.
- **Line 19:** Exemption amount (see Exemption Chart 10A — phased out at high income).
- **Line 20:** Taxable net income = Line 18 − Line 19.

### Standard Deduction (BRFA — New for 2025)

| Filing Status | 2025 Amount | 2024 Amount |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|
| MFJ / HoH / QSS | **$6,700** | $5,450 |
| Single / MFS / Dependent | **$3,350** | $2,725 |

[MD Tax Alert, Section IV.A; BRFA Chapter 604]

- The income-based phase-in has been **eliminated** — the full amount applies regardless of income.
- Future increases will be indexed to cost-of-living adjustments.

### Itemized Deduction for Maryland
- Maryland itemized deductions = **Federal Schedule A itemized deductions MINUS state and local income taxes**. [MD Resident Booklet, Instruction 17]
- You subtract the state and local income tax amounts (Schedule A Lines 5a amounts for state/local income taxes) from your total federal itemized deductions.
- Compare the result to the standard deduction; use whichever is larger.

### Itemized Deduction Phase-Out (BRFA — New for 2025)
- If FAGI exceeds **$200,000** ($100,000 MFS), itemized deductions are reduced by **7.5% of FAGI in excess** of the threshold. [MD Tax Alert, Section IV.B]
- The threshold is $200,000 for **all filing statuses** except MFS ($100,000).
- Breakeven table (MFJ): Itemized deductions must exceed (standard deduction + reduction) to benefit.

| FAGI (MFJ) | Excess over $200K | 7.5% Reduction | Breakeven (must exceed to itemize) |
|------------|-------------------|----------------|--------------------------------------|
| $200,000 | $0 | $0 | $6,700 |
| $250,000 | $50,000 | $3,750 | $10,450 |
| $275,000 | $75,000 | $5,625 | $12,325 |
| $300,000 | $100,000 | $7,500 | $14,200 |
| $400,000 | $200,000 | $15,000 | $21,700 |

[MD Tax Alert, Section IV.B, Example 2]

### Exemptions (Line 19)
- Personal exemption: **$3,200** per person. [MD Resident Booklet, Instruction 10A]
- Phase-out for MFJ:

| FAGI (MFJ) | Exemption per Person |
|------------|---------------------|
| $150,000 or less | $3,200 |
| $150,001 – $175,000 | $1,600 |
| $175,001 – $200,000 | $800 |
| Over $200,000 | **$0** |

- Age/blindness exemption ($1,000 each) is NOT subject to the phase-out.

### State Tax Computation (Line 21)
- Use Tax Rate Schedule II (MFJ) — see 2025-tax-numbers.md for full brackets.
- If taxable net income ≥ $100,000, use the Maryland Tax Computation Worksheet Schedules (17A). [MD Resident Booklet, Instruction 17]

### Capital Gains Surtax (BRFA — New for 2025)
- Additional **2%** tax on net capital gains for individuals with FAGI over **$350,000**. [MD Tax Alert, Section III]
- Subject to certain exceptions.
- Does NOT apply if FAGI ≤ $350,000.

### Local Income Tax (Line 28)
- Counties and Baltimore City levy a local income tax as a percentage of Maryland taxable net income. [MD Resident Booklet, Instruction 19]
- **Montgomery County: 3.20%** [MD Resident Booklet, Local Tax Rate Chart]
- Use Local Tax Worksheet (19A): Line 20 (taxable net income) × local rate.

### Credits
- **Line 22:** Earned income credit (if eligible).
- **Line 23:** State poverty level credit.
- **Line 24:** Other state credits (various — see Form 502CR).
- **Line 29:** Local earned income credit.

### Payments and Balance Due
- **Line 40:** Maryland tax withheld (W-2 Box 17 state income tax + any 1099 withholding).
- **Line 41:** Estimated tax payments (MD Form PV quarterly payments).
- **Line 42:** Other credits (Form 502CR).
- **Line 43:** Total payments and credits.
- **Line 44:** Overpayment (refund) = Line 43 − Line 34 (if positive).
- **Line 46:** Tax due = Line 34 − Line 43 (if positive).
